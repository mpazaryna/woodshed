The class plan should include:

1. A clear introduction to the practice focus
2. Proper warm-up sequence
3. Main pose sequences and transitions
4. Cool-down and meditation guidance
5. Sanskrit names are required for all poses
6. Poses should be named in the following format: "Pose Name (Sanskrit Name)"
7. Poses should flow logically and be anatomical compatible.  
8. Include a section on the philosophy of yoga and how it relates to the practice.
