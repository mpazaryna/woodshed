# vinyasa Yoga Sequence - hip opening and flexibility

## Overview
A flowing practice that gradually opens the hips through progressive poses and movements. The sequence builds heat mindfully while creating space in the hip joints and surrounding muscles, incorporating both dynamic and static stretches to improve flexibility and joint mobility.

## Details
- **Level**: intermediate
- **Duration**: 75 minutes
- **Props Required**: blocks, strap
- **Contraindications**: knee injuries, hip replacements

## Sequence

### Centering and Breath Awareness (4 minutes)
- Easy Seat (Sukhasana)
  - Duration: 4 breaths
  - Cue: Sit tall, close your eyes, and focus on deep, steady breaths.
  - Modification: Sit on a block for added support.

### Warmup and Joint Mobilization (11 minutes)
- Cat-Cow Pose (Marjaryasana-Bitilasana)
  - Duration: 5 breaths
  - Cue: Inhale arching the back, exhale rounding the spine.
  - Modification: Move with a smaller range of motion if needed.
- Downward Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)
  - Duration: 5 breaths
  - Cue: Press through palms, lengthen spine, engage core.
  - Modification: Bend the knees slightly for tight hamstrings.

### Main Sequence (49 minutes)
- Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)
  - Duration: 5 breaths
  - Cue: Align front heel with back arch, extend arms parallel to the floor.
  - Modification: Use a block under the front hand for stability.
- Extended Side Angle (Utthita Parsvakonasana)
  - Duration: 5 breaths
  - Cue: Reach the top arm overhead, lengthen the side body.
  - Modification: Rest the bottom forearm on a block.
- Pigeon Pose (Eka Pada Rajakapotasana)
  - Duration: 8 breaths per side
  - Cue: Square hips, flex front foot, hinge at the hips.
  - Modification: Place a block under the hip of the bent leg.
- Bound Angle Pose (Baddha Konasana)
  - Duration: 5 breaths
  - Cue: Press soles of feet together, lengthen spine.
  - Modification: Sit on a block for added support.

### Cooldown (7 minutes)
- Seated Forward Fold (Paschimottanasana)
  - Duration: 5 breaths
  - Cue: Hinge at hips, lengthen spine, fold forward.
  - Modification: Bend the knees if hamstrings are tight.

### Final Relaxation (4 minutes)
- Savasana
  - Duration: 4 minutes
  - Cue: Relax the entire body, let go of tension, and focus on the breath.
  - Modification: Place a bolster under the knees for lower back support.

Remember to flow smoothly through the transitions, maintaining a focus on breath and alignment throughout the practice. Enjoy the hip-opening journey!
