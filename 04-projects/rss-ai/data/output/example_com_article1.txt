- OpenAI has developed a new series of AI reasoning models that spend more time thinking before responding, excelling in solving complex problems in science, coding, math, and more.
- These models, named OpenAI o1, have enhanced reasoning capabilities and safety training to adhere to guidelines effectively, making them suitable for users tackling complex tasks.
- Besides the o1 model, OpenAI has also released a faster and cheaper model called OpenAI o1-mini, optimized for coding tasks and offering a cost-effective solution for specific applications.
