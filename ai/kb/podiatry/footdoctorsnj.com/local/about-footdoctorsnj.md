# Affiliated Foot and Ankle Center, LLP

## About Affiliated Foot and Ankle Center

The podiatrists at Affiliated Foot and Ankle Center, LLP  treat foot and ankle problems. We treat everyone from infants to seniors, providing both non-surgical and surgical options for foot and ankle conditions. We specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting the foot and ankle such as wound care, heel and arch pain, bunions, hammertoes, ingrown nails, sports injuries and foot care associated with diabetes.

If you're suffering with foot or ankle pain, we want you to know we're here to help. We will take the time to listen to you, and together, we will determine the best course of treatment for your lifestyle. Give our friendly office staff a call Howell Office 732-905-1110. They will answer all of your questions and get you right in for an appointment.
